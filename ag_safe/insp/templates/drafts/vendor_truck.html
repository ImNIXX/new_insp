Vendor Truck Loading and Unloading Checklist
Complete the following checklist to assist in planning and to communicate the safety requirements to the truck driver/operator prior to loading or unloading activities.
Equipment / Load Information
Physical Location/Hazards:
Is the truck bed wet or icy?
Is there dirt or debris on the truck bed?
Do chain binders contain stored energy?
Do ratchet straps contain stored energy?
Does the equipment or material have sharp edges or splinters?
Did the load shift during transit?
Are weather conditions a factor?
Are ground conditions at the loading/unloading area muddy, icy or uneven?
Are there overhead lines in the loading/unloading area?
Is there other work in the nearby area?
Are there any other hazards?
Personal Protective Equipment
Gloves, hard hat, safety boots and safety glasses with side shields
Face shield (required when removing chain binders, ratchet straps and tie down rigging)
Reflective vest or coveralls with day/night reflective striping
