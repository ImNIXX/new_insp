Summon emergency services as follows:
Step Action
1 In the event of an emergency,worker(s)will summon help by sounding horn or other sounding device to stop work.
2 Caller identifies:
  • Who they are
  • Emergency location
  • Nature of emergency
  • Casualties
3 Caller to remain online for further instructions
4 Additional workers to provide assistance to:
  • Injured personnel
  • Attempt a non-entry rescue if safe to do so and trained
  • Direct emergency response personnel
  • Secure incident scene including equipment and material
5 Emergency services to assess the situation,review this plan and establish procedures for rescue.
6 Emergency services assess the need for mutual aid if required.
Summoning of emergency services is complete.
During an emergency secure the confined space as follows:
Step Action
1 Secure scene and establish hot zone and warm zone,consider Security contractor for policing zones
2 Isolate and de-energized systems
3 Gas test and continuous monitoring,CSV as required
4 Determine appropriate egress route
5 Consider the location of anchor points
6 Consider evacuating of the all confined space workers
Confined space is now secure.
