QUALIFICATIONS REQUIRMENTS

The following requirements have been confirmed for this operation:

  • Has the required physical and mental abilities to operate the equipment
  • Has reviewed and demonstrates an understanding of operator's manual, including operating restrictions and hazards
  • Has reviewed site safety rules and procedures (i.e.: work in the vicinity of power lines)
  • Understands all phases of setting up and operating the unit
  • Has received and successfully completed specific written and/or hands-on training and instruction on the equipment to be operated
  • Has demonstrated proficiency in the safe operation of the AWP to be used
