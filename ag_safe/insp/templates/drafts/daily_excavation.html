Daily Excavation and Trench Inspection Report
INSPECT ALL OPEN TRENCHES AS PER THE FOLLOWING CRITERIA: Yes No N/A
1 Is the spoil pile located a proper distance from the excavation (min. 1 meter)?
2 Is there any tension cracks observed along the top of any slopes?
3 Is the trench slopes cut at the design angle of 45 degrees?
4 Was there any water seepage noted in trench walls or bottom?
5 Was the installation of the bracing system complete in accordance with design?
6 Is there any evidence of caving or sloughing of soil since the last inspection?
7 Were there any zones of unusually weak soils or materials not anticipated?
8 Is there adequate egress ladders provided (within 8 meters / 25 feet of work) maximum 50 feet apart?
9 Are trench box(s) certified? (If applicable)
10 If shoring is used, is it properly secure?
11 If shoring is used, is it the shoring of the correct type?
12 Are the hydraulic shores pumped to the design pressure (if applicable)?
13 Did shoring plan include an adequate safety factor to allow for specific equipment?
14 Are barricades in place to keep traffic away from trenching operations?
15 Are there trees, boulders or other hazards in the area?
16 Are vibrations from equipment or traffic too close to trenching operations?
17 Is a safety standby person required?
18 Is a confined space entry permit required?
